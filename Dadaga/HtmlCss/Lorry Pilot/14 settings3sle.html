Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center. Find answers to your questions below or use the search bar for quick results.

Getting Started

How do I set up my emergency contacts?

From the main Safety Settings screen, tap "Add Contact." You can add phone numbers for family, friends, or a manager.

How do I link my app to a company dispatcher?

Go to the Contacts page from the main menu and select "Company/Dispatcher." Enter the required details to share your location and status with your fleet manager.

Why is my location not updating?

First, check your phone's GPS settings to ensure location services are enabled for the app. If the issue persists, check your data connection. Location updates require a stable internet connection.

SOS & Alerts

What is "Silent SOS"?

Silent SOS sends a discreet alert to your emergency contacts without making a sound or showing an on-screen notification. This is for situations where making noise could be dangerous.

What triggers a "Fatigue Alert Reminder"?

This alert is based on your trip duration and driving patterns. You can customize the alert frequency in the Safety Settings menu to best fit your driving schedule.

Location Sharing

How do I share my live location?

Go to the Share Live Location page. Choose who to share with (Dispatcher, family, etc.) and select a duration (e.g., "for trip duration" or "for a fixed time"). Tap "Start Sharing."

How do I stop sharing my location?

You can stop sharing at any time by simply returning to the Share Live Location page and tapping the "Stop Sharing" button.

Troubleshooting

My app is not sending alerts. What's wrong?

Check your app permissions in your phone's settings. Ensure the app has permission to send notifications and access your contacts.

What if I lose my phone?

Immediately contact our support team. We can help secure your account and prevent unauthorized use of your safety features.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal and location data. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it to provide our safety services.

What We Collect

We only collect data necessary to make this app function as a safety tool. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, and emergency contact details.
  • Location Data: Your GPS coordinates, but only when you have activated a safety feature like live location sharing or SOS alerts.
  • Device Information: Your phone's model and operating system to help us fix bugs and improve performance.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Send SOS alerts and location data to your pre-set emergency contacts and the police in a crisis.
  • Allow you to securely share your location with your company dispatcher or family.
  • Provide you with helpful alerts based on your trip (e.g., Fatigue Alert).
  • Improve the app's performance and fix technical issues.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your data is stored on secure servers and is never sold to third parties.

Your Control

You are always in control of your data.

  • You can stop sharing your live location at any time.
  • You can edit or delete your emergency contacts.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting our support team.
