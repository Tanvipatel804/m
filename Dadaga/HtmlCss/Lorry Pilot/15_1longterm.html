Help Center

Getting Started

How do I set up emergency contacts?

From Safety Settings, tap "Add Contact" to add phone numbers for family or a manager.

Why is my location not updating?

Check your phone's GPS settings and ensure location services are enabled. Location updates also require a stable internet connection.

SOS & Alerts

What is "Silent SOS"?

Silent SOS sends a discreet alert to emergency contacts without sound — ideal for dangerous situations.

Location Sharing

How do I stop sharing my location?

Return to the Share Live Location page and tap "Stop Sharing" at any time.

Privacy & Security

What We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, emergency contacts.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, only when a safety feature is active.
  • Device Information: Phone model and OS for bug fixes.

How We Use Your Data

  • Send SOS alerts and location to emergency contacts and police.
  • Allow secure location sharing with your dispatcher or family.
  • Provide helpful alerts based on your trip.

Your Control

  • Stop location sharing at any time.
  • Edit or delete emergency contacts.
  • Delete your account by contacting support.
