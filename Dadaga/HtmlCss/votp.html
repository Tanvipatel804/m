⚡ Driver Onboarding Flow
1
Transporter adds Truck
Registers VIN, plate, type, capacity, and all compliance documents to company account.
2
Driver gets Invite Link
Unique invite code sent via SMS/WhatsApp. Driver creates a personal profile — auto-linked to the transporter's company.
3
Assignment & Check-In
Transporter assigns Driver A to Truck B. Driver "checks in" from the app. Truck turns green on dispatcher's map.
4
Trip is Live
Driver sees only their route. Transporter sees the full fleet. All status updates flow in real-time between both.

Transporter (Admin)
Web Dashboard / Pro App
Sees all 14 trucks on live map with GPS trails
Assigns any driver to any idle truck in 2 taps
Manages document vault: insurance, permits, fitness certs
Dispatches new loads and pushes to driver's phone instantly
Monitors Hours of Service for all drivers to avoid violations
Sees revenue, trip history, and driver performance scores
Broadcasts alerts to entire fleet or individual drivers
📱
Driver (Operator)
Simplified Mobile App
Sees only their assigned truck and active trip details
Turn-by-turn navigation to pickup and delivery point
One-tap status updates: Picked Up / In Transit / Delivered
Views own digital documents (license, permits) in one place
Checks personal HOS remaining hours for the day
In-app chat with dispatcher only — not with other drivers
Cannot see other drivers' locations, pay, or assignments
